Our Story

Rooted in Craft,
Driven by Curiosity

Ember & Oak started as a small weekend roasting project in a South Austin garage. Ten years later we've grown into a full roastery and café — but our approach hasn't changed one bit.

We travel to origin farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala every year to build real relationships with the growers who make our coffee possible. Every bag you drink tells that story.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you offer whole bean bags to take home?

+

Yes! All our beans are available in-store and we also offer local pickup orders via our website. We roast every Tuesday and Friday so beans are never more than a few days old.

Is there parking available near the café?

+

There's free street parking along South Lamar and a small lot behind our building with 8 spaces. We're also very bikeable — we have racks out front and a covered lock-up in back.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

+

Absolutely. We stock oat milk, almond milk, and coconut milk as standard alternatives at no extra charge. Most of our food items can be adapted — just ask your barista.

Can I host a private event at Ember & Oak?

+

Yes! We host private cuppings, team offsites, and intimate gatherings for up to 25 people. Reach out via the contact form or call us to check availability.

Do you offer brewing classes or workshops?

+

We run monthly pour-over workshops on the first Saturday of each month. They're $35 per person and cover fundamentals of brewing, grind size, and water temperature.

Is there WiFi and space to work?

+

We have fast, free WiFi and plenty of table space. During peak morning hours (8–10am) we ask that large work setups stay to the back seating area so everyone has room.
