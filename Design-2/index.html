Our Story

Rooted in Craft,
Brewed with Heart

Ember & Oak began in 2016 as a small weekend roasting project in a garage off 100 Feet Road. We had one drum roaster, zero budget, and an obsession with how different Indian and international beans could taste with the right care.

Today we're a full roastery and café — but our north star hasn't moved. We travel to estates in Coorg, Chikmagalur, and Araku every season. We also source direct from farms in Ethiopia and Colombia. Every bag you buy funds that relationship.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you ship beans across India?

+

Yes! We ship PAN India via courier. Orders placed before 12pm on Tuesday or Thursday ship the same day as a fresh roast batch. Typically 2–4 days delivery.

Is there parking near the café?

+

There's street parking on 12th Main and a paid parking lot 200m away. We're very walkable from the Indiranagar metro station — just a 5-minute walk.

Do you have plant-based milk options?

+

Absolutely. We stock oat milk and almond milk at no extra charge. Our in-house oat milk is made fresh every morning from rolled oats.

Can I host a private event or corporate tasting?

+

Yes! We host private cuppings, team offsites, and intimate gatherings for up to 20 guests. Reach out via WhatsApp or call us to check availability and pricing.

Do you offer brewing workshops?

+

We run monthly pour-over workshops every first Saturday. They're ₹1,200 per person and cover grind size, water temperature, and brew ratios. DM us to register.

Is there WiFi for working from the café?

+

Yes — fast, free WiFi throughout. During peak hours (8–11am) we request that screens stay to the back seating area so everyone has a comfortable space.
